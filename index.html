Why is Tabless Thursday awesome?

Tabless Thursday helps users stay organized, stay focused, and be more collaborative.

Stay Organized

Tabless Thursday keeps you organized by allowing you to save tabs to custom named tab folders for later.

Stay Focused

Tabless Thursday helps keep you focused by keeping you from having to dig around for the links to important things.

Collaborate

Tabless Thursday helps you and your team be more collaborative by allowing users to share and invite team members to tab organizations.
